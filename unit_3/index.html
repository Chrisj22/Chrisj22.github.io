About me

I specialise in creating contemporary handcrafted wedding cakes from my Sussex studio.

Having had a flair for design and a love of baking from an early age, I've always been passionate about cake and creating delicacies that are both elegant and delicious. I pride myself on my meticulous attention to detail and am keen to ensure that each of my cakes reflects this.

Using only the finest quality local ingredients, I ensure that all my cakes taste exceptional. I enjoy collaborating with every couple I meet to create something truly memorable for their special day.
